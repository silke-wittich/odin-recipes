Pasta Pesto

A classic pasta pesto dish with some additional nutrition.

This is a quick and easy to make pasta pesto dish with some protein and vegetables to add some color and flavor to your plate. Perfect for days when you need a quick and fulfilling meal, or if you just don't feel like spending a long time cooking.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Genovese Basil Pesto
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Zucchini
  • Yellow Onion
  • Red Bell Peppers
  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Italian Spices

Instructions

  1. Start boiling water for your pasta with a dash of salt and olive oil.
  2. Cut your vegetables and chicken into blocks while preheating a pan on medium.
  3. Add vegetables and chicken to the pan.
  4. Add salt, pepper, chilli flakes, and Italian spices to the pan to taste.
  5. Take a tablespoon of pesto and add it to the chicken and vegetables.
  6. Once the pasta is finished, remove from heat and pour out water.
  7. Add pasto to the pasta (to taste), chicken and veggies, and mix.
  8. Enjoy.
